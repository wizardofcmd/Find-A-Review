About Us

Based on our existing competitors, ‘FindABook’ seeks to fill the opened gap by delivering a modern take through SaaS (Software as a Service). Our objective is to provide an interface for users to find books based on criteria they choose. This will be achieved with a contemporary and minimalist design, taking advantage of what our competitors are lacking in terms of comfortable UX. Why would someone use this? This site simply provides books, reviews, and also suggests similar books. Its simplistic and straightforward nature exceeds what is currently on the market.
